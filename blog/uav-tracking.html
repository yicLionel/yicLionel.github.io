Tracking Vehicles from UAV Footage

Vehicle tracking from UAV cameras is difficult because scale, background clutter, and illumination can change quickly.

The research explored CamShift, MeanShift, HSV colour components, search windows, and two-dimensional histograms for improving tracking robustness.

The main lesson is that practical perception systems need both visual features and runtime constraints to be considered together.
