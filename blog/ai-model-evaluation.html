From AI Model Output to Analyst Framework

AI model evaluation becomes more useful when qualitative outputs are converted into repeatable scoring dimensions.

For AI agents and video-generation models, useful dimensions include reasoning, instruction following, multimodal ability, content quality, and commercial applicability.

A structured analyst framework makes model comparison easier to communicate and easier to update as products change.
